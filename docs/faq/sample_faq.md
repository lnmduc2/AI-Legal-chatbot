# AI Legal FAQ - Information Security & Data Privacy

### Q: Can I use my personal laptop for work?

A: Personal devices must not be used for work purposes unless essential and with explicit ISM Manager permission. If approved, the device must be checked by IT, with proper antivirus and endpoint control installed. Refer to the "Personal owned devices" section of the ISMS policy.

### Q: Can I work from home without approval?

A: No. Teleworking and working from home are only allowed with ISM Manager approval and for a defined duration. You are accountable for information security while working remotely. Refer to the "Teleworking rules" section.

### Q: Can I share customer data in public cloud tools (Google Drive, ChatGPT, etc.)?

A: No. Do not upload or store Company's confidential information into any public or personal website or web storage. Do not use online tools or public websites/storage to upload or translate documents containing Confidential information. Refer to the "Information Assets" and "Network Security Management" sections.

### Q: What should I do if I suspect a security incident?

A: Report to your Manager or ISM Manager immediately. You should also contribute knowledge and experience to protect, fix, and prevent incidents. Refer to the "Security incident management" section.

### Q: What confidentiality levels exist and how should I handle documents?

A: There are 4 levels: TOP CONFIDENTIAL, CONFIDENTIAL, INTERNAL USE, and PUBLIC. By default, information is INTERNAL USE in your Unit/Project. Do not share Internal documents outside of Unit/Project, and do not share COMPANY INTERNAL documents outside the Company. Refer to the "CONFIDENTIALITY" section.

### Q: Can I use USB drives to transfer files?

A: No. Removable media such as USB drives and removable disks are prohibited. If you must transfer media, it must be encrypted during transportation. Refer to the "MEDIA HANDLING" section.

### Q: What should I do if a privacy issue is not clearly covered by policy?

A: In cases not listed in the Regulations, you must ask your Manager or ISM Manager how to act. Refer to the "All other rules" section.

### Q: Can I use my personal email or social media for work purposes?

A: Do not use Company emails for non-work purposes. Do not use Company emails to register to any forums or social websites. Do not post, upload, share, or disclose internal/confidential information on social media. Refer to the "Transferring information, Email" and "Social media" sections.

### Q: How should I handle passwords?

A: You must keep your passwords totally secret. Do not lend or borrow Company accounts and passwords. Follow the IT department policy for password creation and change. Do not search or guess others' passwords. Refer to the "Access of networks and network services" section.

### Q: What happens if I violate information security rules?

A: Violations may result in bonus deductions, mandatory ISMS re-training, impact on task-completion assessment, compensation for damage, and disciplinary actions per Company Labor regulations. Refer to the "Discipline for violation" section.

### Q: Do I need to attend information security training?

A: Yes. You must attend the Company's information security training on the effective day of your labor contract, and attend refresh training at least once per year. Refer to the "Human Resource rules" section.
